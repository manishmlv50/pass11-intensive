11 Plus Intensive Summer Courses for a Final Boost

Our 11 plus intensive summer courses are designed to give students a focused and effective revision boost ahead of their grammar school exams. These short-term programmes are scheduled during school holidays, making them ideal for concentrated preparation without ongoing school pressure.

Across our 11 plus intensive courses, students revisit key topics in Maths, English, Verbal Reasoning, and Non-Verbal Reasoning, ensuring strong concept clarity and exam readiness. Each 11 plus summer intensive course is delivered by experienced tutors in small class settings, allowing for personalised attention and targeted support.

✓Targeted revision across Maths, English, VR & NVR
✓Timed mock tests with detailed, actionable feedback
✓Exam-day strategies: pacing, time management & calm
✓Maximum 10 students per group
✓Available online and in person in Birmingham & Edgbaston

Personalised guidance, small class sizes and expert feedback, so students feel prepared, confident and ready to perform at their best.
